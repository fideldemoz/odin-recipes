Ingredients:

  • 8 oz. of spaghetti or any pasta of your choice
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes (14.5 oz.)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes with their juice to the skillet, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Simmer the sauce for 5-7 minutes until it has thickened slightly.
  6. Drain the pasta and add it to the skillet with the tomato sauce. Toss until the pasta is well coated.
  7. Serve the pasta hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves (if using).

Enjoy your simple and delicious pasta dish!
